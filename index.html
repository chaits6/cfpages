Experience with Cloudflare

Using Cloudflare significantly enhanced my website’s speed, performance, and security, demonstrating the effectiveness of Cloudflare’s free features and easy-to-use platform. This experience highlighted Cloudflare’s potential as a robust solution for website optimization and protection.

Cloudflare’s UI was easy to navigate, and the free plan covered all the essential functions needed for my project. The free plan covers essential functions, making it accessible for initial projects and small websites.

The Cloudflare carbon savings feature was a cherry on the top. Cloudflare's user-friendly dashboard provides easy access to tools for traffic analytics, threat monitoring, and domain management.
